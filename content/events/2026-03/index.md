We'll be discussing [Wake: Tales from the Aqualab (2023)](https://fielddaylab.wisc.edu/play/wake/) by [Field Day](https://fielddaylab.wisc.edu/). Play as a young scientist exploring ocean ecosystems and conducting experiments based on real research. The game was a [finalist for the 2024 Games for Change Awards (Best in Environmental Impact)](https://education.wisc.edu/news/media-literacy-game-from-uw-madisons-field-day-lab-wins-big-at-games-for-change-awards/).

*Wake* is [open source](https://github.com/fielddaylab/wake), makes its [gameplay data available for research](https://opengamedata.fielddaylab.wisc.edu/gamedata.php?game=AQUALAB), and provides [resources for teachers](https://sites.google.com/wisc.edu/waketeacherguide/curriculum).
