About Night of the Living Debt

The iPad version of Night of the Living Debt won several awards including:

  • "Best Overall Digital Game" at the Meaningful Play Conference (2016)
  • Gold in the International Serious Play Awards (2016)
  • The Association for Communications Excellence (ACE) Gold Award for Interactive Media Program (2017)

It's one of the 10 personal finance games that have been developed as a part of the Northwest Youth Financial Education project.

The paper A Model for Youth Financial Education in Extension Involving a Game-Based Approach (2019) contains additional details about the game and its use.
