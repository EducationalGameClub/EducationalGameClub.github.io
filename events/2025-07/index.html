About Rogue Story

Rogue Story is a side project by FranÃ§ois to explore how recent developments in various AI technologies could both 1) enable new kinds of gameplay and 2) transform game development processes, tools, and workflows. A key part of the discussion will be critiquing and improving how the game teaches new mechanics.
