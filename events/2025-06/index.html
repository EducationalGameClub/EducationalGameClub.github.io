About Geniventure

Geniventure is designed for use in a classroom with an instructor. It has an extensive set of teaching materials including:

  • An online course for instructors on how to teach using the game.
  • Out-of-school time materials which include hands-on activities and labs. They provide curriculum options for one week and six week programs.
  • A pre-quiz and a post-quiz to assess student understanding before and after playing the game.
